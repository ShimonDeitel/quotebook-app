Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Quotebook - Quote Journal is an offline-first app. All of your data (entries you add, ratings, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device using the operating system's application support storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in this app.

The only network communication in this app is with Apple's App Store / StoreKit servers for processing purchases and validating your Pro subscription/purchase entitlement, which is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed from your device.
